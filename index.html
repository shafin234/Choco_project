Our Story

Gazipur, Dhaka

From our humble beginnings in the vibrant heart of Gazipur, Dhaka, Choco. has grown into a premier destination for luxury chocolate experiences. We believe that true indulgence is born from passion, precision, and the finest ingredients sourced with care.

Our prospect is simple yet ambitious: to redefine the art of chocolate-making in Bangladesh. Every piece we craft is a testament to our dedication to unparalleled quality, transforming simple cocoa into a masterpiece meant to be savored. Discover the rich heritage and innovative spirit that make Choco. truly exceptional.
